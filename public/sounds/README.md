# Notification Sounds

Place your notification sound files here:

- `boat-horn.mp3` - Deep boat horn sound for boat matches (⚓)
- `airplane-ding.mp3` - Airplane seatbelt ding for plane matches (✈)
- `foghorn.mp3` - Short foghorn for new messages
- `cash-register.mp3` - Cash register cha-ching for counter-offers

## For Native Apps

For iOS and Android native builds, you'll need `.wav` versions:

- `boat_horn.wav` - Copy to `ios/App/App/` and `android/app/src/main/res/raw/`
- `airplane_ding.wav` - Copy to `ios/App/App/` and `android/app/src/main/res/raw/`
- `foghorn.wav` - Copy to `ios/App/App/` and `android/app/src/main/res/raw/`
- `cash_register.wav` - Copy to `ios/App/App/` and `android/app/src/main/res/raw/`

## Sound Requirements

- **Format**: MP3 for web, WAV for native
- **Duration**: 1-3 seconds recommended
- **Volume**: Normalized to avoid clipping
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz recommended

- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org) - Search for "boat horn", "airplane ding", "foghorn", "cash register"
